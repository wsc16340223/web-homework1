Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-inch pie
30 Min - Prep time
10 Min - Cook time
40 Min - Total
8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cuo white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons,juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 egg yolks,beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tablespoons white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Make Lemon Filling: In a medium saucepan ...
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar,flour,cornstarch,and salt.
    • Stir in water,lemon juice and lemon zest.
    • Cook over medium-high heat,strring frequently,until mixture comes to a boil.
    • Stir in butter.
    • Place egg yolks in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
    • Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar minture.
    • Bring to a boil and continue to cook while strring constantly until thick.
